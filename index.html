Dead Dice

This version of Dead Dice is a simple 2 player game of chance. The object of the game is to score the most points. In order to score points, each player rolls five dice. If any of the dice land on two or five, they get discarded, and the rest of the pips on the remaining die get counted towards the players points. Ones all dice have been discarded, points are compared to determine the winner.
